Poem: Hold My Hand 🤝

Hold my hand,
not just for now,
but until time forgets our names
and the stars no longer shine.

Hold it through storms,
through silence,
through the soft fading of our days.

And when the fire of life dims,
when we turn to ashes,
let our hands still speak
what words could never hold,
forever.

Poem: Ten Years From Now 💌

Ten years from now,
I want to see myself loving the same person I love today,
with the same smile,
the same spark that started it all.

I dream of mornings with the same soul,
coffee in hand, hearts at ease,
you beside me then, now, and always.

Not because it’s easy,
but because it’s you.
And loving you…
was never just a phase.

And if the universe allows,
I hope it's still you,
the one my heart knew
before time ever asked it to decide.
